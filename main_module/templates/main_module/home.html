Why Us?

If you want to work with a team that truly cares about your project and is committed to delivering outstanding results, choose us. Let us show you what sets you apart from the rest. Looking for a team of experts to bring your 3D modeling, lighting, rendering, animation, and VFX projects to life? Look no further! Our team boasts a wealth of experience and expertise in these fields, making us the perfect choice for anyone looking to work with the best in the business. Whether you're looking to create stunning visual effects or need high-quality 3D visualization for your next project, we've got you covered. So why wait? Contact us today and let's get started!

Our Process

Let us describe the process of a project with us: First, by consulting with you, your needs and desires will be listed in your work area, and if possible, we will provide advice from our side. The second step is to present a concept of the architectural plan or a scenario of your desired animation. The third step is to start modeling according to the needs of your project. Models come to your approval during the execution. The fourth step (in animation projects) is to create a preview output. In these outputs, the quality is low and the materials are not displayed well. At this step, the work is technically approved by you. The fifth step will be a low-quality rendering to confirm the light and material. In animation projects, a rendering sample is provided from each sequence. The sixth step is the final approval by you and the start of the rendering process. This step is the point of no return and due to the high cost of the next steps, after approval, the previous steps will not be editable. The seventh step is the post-production process for the final output. After preparing the final output, a low-quality version will be delivered to you so that you can give us your writing in each part of the animation (Infographic). In single-frame renderings, you can provide the necessary information to be written on the work if necessary.

Guarantee

To guarantee work, our payment process is in such a way that you bear the least sense of risk The first payment is only 10% of the project from you. This amount guarantees that you are serious about doing the project. The second stage of payment is before the final rendering and after seeing the previews and technical approval of the work from your side. Where about 80% of the project has progressed, and the project is ready for final output. The final 50% of the amount will be paid by you after the completion of the project and final approval. All our efforts in this process are to gain your trust in our quality and responsibility toward you.
